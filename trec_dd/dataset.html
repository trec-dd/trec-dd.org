Ebola

This data is related to the Ebola outbreak in Africa in 2014-2015. The dataset comprises 30 million tweets relating to the outbreak, and 500,000 web pages from sites hosted in the affected countries and designed to provide information to citizens and aid workers on the ground.

The Ebola dataset has three distinct subcollections: tweets, webpages, and PDFs
